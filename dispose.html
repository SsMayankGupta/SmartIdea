Dispose Smartly

Small actions today create a cleaner tomorrow.

♻️

Plastic Waste

  • Rinse bottles before disposal
  • Flatten plastic containers
  • Avoid mixing with food waste
  • Prefer reusable bags
🍃

Organic (Wet) Waste

  • Separate kitchen waste daily
  • Use compost bins
  • Avoid plastic bags
  • Convert to manure
📱

E-Waste

  • Never throw in dustbins
  • Use authorized centers
  • Remove batteries
  • Donate usable items


🧱

Construction Waste

  • Avoid roadside dumping
  • Reuse bricks if possible
  • Contact municipality
  • Store debris safely
🗑️

General Waste

  • Use covered bins
  • Do not mix hazardous waste
  • Dispose daily
  • Reduce consumption
